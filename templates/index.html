OUR PURPOSE

Nestled beside the Pacific Rim National Park in Ucluelet, British Columbia, Canada, in front of the crashing waves of the majestic Pacific Ocean, The Nami Project was created for the purpose of a personal escape or just the destination of the wanderlust desire of a traveller. A unique ocean side fortress of serenity built for a calming or invigorating escape experience.
